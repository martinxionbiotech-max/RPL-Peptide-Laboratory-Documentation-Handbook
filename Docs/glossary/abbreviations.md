## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Where can I find the full form of an abbreviation on a COA?**
A: Most abbreviations are defined in the footer or header of each COA. This glossary provides the complete reference.

**Q: Are all abbreviations standard?**
A: Yes — all abbreviations follow ICH, USP, and pharmacopoeial conventions unless otherwise noted.
