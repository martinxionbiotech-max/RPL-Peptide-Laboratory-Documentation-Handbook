### Standard Production Workflow

1. **Peptide Synthesis**
   Solid-Phase Peptide Synthesis (SPPS) is used to manufacture peptide sequences according to defined production specifications.

2. **Purification Process**
   Crude peptides undergo purification using preparative HPLC systems to achieve the required purity targets.

3. **Analytical Verification**
   Qualified batches may be evaluated through HPLC purity testing, LC-MS molecular weight confirmation, and internal quality review procedures.

4. **Lyophilization & Packaging**
   Finished materials are processed through lyophilization, vial filling, labeling, and packaging based on product and customer requirements.

5. **Documentation & Export Support**
   Batch records, quality documentation, and export-related materials are prepared to support international distribution and long-term supply programs.

## Quality & Traceability

Quality control is integrated into every stage of production, not only final testing.

### Batch Documentation & Controls
Each batch may include:

* **Certificates of Analysis (COA)** for qualified products
* **Raw material verification** and audit trails
* **In-process quality control** monitoring
* **HPLC purity verification** & **LC-MS identity confirmation**
* **Full batch traceability records**
