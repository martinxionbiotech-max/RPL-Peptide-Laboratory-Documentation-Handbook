# Frequently Asked Questions — Retatrutide

## Product Information

### What is retatrutide?
Retatrutide is a synthetic 39-amino-acid triple receptor agonist peptide for research involving GLP-1R, GIPR, and GCGR signaling pathways.

### What is the CAS number?
**2381089-83-2**

### What makes retatrutide different from tirzepatide?
Retatrutide adds glucagon receptor (GCGR) activation to the GIP/GLP-1 dual agonist profile, enabling triple pathway investigation.

## Quality & Documentation

### What purity does RPL supply?
≥99.0% by HPLC at 214 nm.

### Do you provide COA?

## Handling & Storage

### How should retatrutide be stored?
-20°C (2+ years) or 2–8°C (12 months). Protect from light and moisture.

### Recommended reconstitution solvent?
Bacteriostatic water (0.9% benzyl alcohol).

### Can I vortex?
No — gently swirl only.
