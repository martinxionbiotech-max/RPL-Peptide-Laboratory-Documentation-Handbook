## 5. Quality Assurance

RPL Peptide ensures the following quality control measures for all Semaglutide batches:

- **Purity Analysis:** Reverse-phase HPLC with UV detection at 214 nm and 280 nm
- **Mass Confirmation:** LC-MS for intact molecular weight determination
- **Sequence Confirmation:** MS/MS fragmentation analysis
- **Water Content:** Karl Fischer titration
- **Endotoxin Testing:** LAL gel-clot method
- **Appearance:** Visual inspection
- **Residual TFA:** Ion chromatography (where applicable)
- **Peptide Content:** Amino acid analysis or UV quantitation

Each batch is accompanied by a Certificate of Analysis (COA) detailing all measured parameters.

## 6. Reconstitution Protocol

| Step | Action |
|------|--------|
| 1 | Equilibrate the vial to room temperature for 15 minutes |
| 2 | Gently tap the vial to ensure all powder settles at the bottom |
| 3 | Remove flip-off cap and disinfect the rubber septum with 70% isopropanol |
| 4 | Slowly inject sterile water for injection, PBS, or sterile saline along the vial wall |
| 5 | Swirl gently — **do not vortex** — until completely dissolved |
| 6 | If needed, further dilute in appropriate buffer for the specific assay |
